About Me

I was born and raised in Torrance,California. My birthday is on March 9th,1992 and I'm 25 years old. I am of Japanese descent but I can't speak it!
I'm 3rd generation and my parents weren't able to teach me, but one of these days I will eventually learn. One of my dreams is to travel the globe and experience what the world has to offer.

Some of my hobbies include playing basketball, playing computer games, and going hiking. Ever since I was a kid, I loved the Lakers.
Kobe Bryant was my favorite player growing up and I attended his last game ever in which he scored 60 points! It will forever be one of my greatest memories.
